Talk on Swarm Robotics

Prof. Jishnu Keshavan

Swarm robotics uses numerous, autonomous robotics to accomplish a task. Robot swarms coordinate the behaviors of a large number of relatively simple robots in a decentralized manner. Prof. Jishnu Keshavan, Assistant Professor at the IISc, is one of the leading and active researchers in robotics. He has extensive research experience and has published several papers on Dynamics and control, Autonomous vision, Unmanned Systems, and many other topics.

    Join us on a journey across the endless possibilities and applications of swarm robotics as we begin the quest of learning early by listening to his ideas and thoughts.
