Crypto-Combat*

Crypto-Combat is a virtual crypto trading battle held as a part of Synergy. It will be a two-day intra-college competition with the goal of instilling a trading habit in college students. To get you started, the co-founder of Artemis Network, Praneeth Nagu, one of the IIITB's respected alumni, will be addressing a brief presentation on cryptocurrency trading at the beginning. He is an avid trader who participates in a variety of marketplaces.

      Grab this opportunity to rid the trading world of dread. The most profitable traders will win cash prizes worth Rs 10,000 at the end of the competition.
