Blockchain 101 on Tezos

Workshop by Tezos India

Blockchain has introduced an entirely new set of possibilities across society. Use cases are abundant and diverse: ranging from charity to food labels to social media to utilities. Synergy workshop series presents to you a one-of-a-kind perspective on “decentralized open-source blockchain” conducted by Tezos.

    This workshop will be taken by Ojuswi Rastogi. He is a blockchain developer from Tezos India with experience in Python and Django. He has contributed to various opensource events like the MLH pre-fellowship and the GSSoC.
